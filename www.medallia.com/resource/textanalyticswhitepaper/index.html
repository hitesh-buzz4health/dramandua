Medallia’s Approach to Text Analytics

Whitepaper

Share this resource

The last decade has seen a explosion in the variety of ways customers provide feedback to businesses. This feedback includes both quantitative and qualitative information.

Quantitative data is structured, and relatively easier to process. Unstructured free-form text data, however, poses more of a challenge — to process efficiently, to combine with other kinds of feedback, and to distribute efficiently across an organization.
This whitepaper explores how Text Analytics technologies can overcome this challenge, taking a wealth of unstructured data and using it to drive action and improvement across a business.
